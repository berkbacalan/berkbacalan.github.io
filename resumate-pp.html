Privacy Policy & Disclaimer

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

ResuMate ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclosure, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Personal Data

We may collect personally identifiable information, such as your name and email address, when you sign in using third-party authentication services (Apple or Google).

1.2. User Content (CV & Job Data)

To provide our core service, we process:

  • The Resume/CV documents you upload (PDF/Text).
  • The personal details contained within your CV (Education, Experience, Contact Info).
  • The Job Description text or URLs you provide.

1.3. Usage & Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous data about how you interact with the App (e.g., feature usage, crash reports) to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information & AI Processing

2.1. AI Service Providers (Model Agnostic Approach)

We use third-party Artificial Intelligence (AI) services to analyze and optimize your CV. These providers may include, but are not limited to, Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic (Claude), DeepSeek, or other future AI technologies.

  • Data Processing: Your text data is sent to these providers solely for the purpose of generating the optimized content.
  • No Training: We utilize APIs where providers generally do not use your data to train their models (subject to their respective API policies).

2.2. Service Delivery

We use your data to generate keyword-optimized resume suggestions and comparison reports.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • AI Service Providers: As described above, for content generation.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Google Firebase (for authentication, database, and backend logic).
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics for Firebase.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Retention: We store your optimized results history linked to your account.
  • Deletion: You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time via the "Delete Account" button in the App Settings. This action is irreversible.
