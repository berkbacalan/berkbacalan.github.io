Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 9, 2026

At AnyScanner, we value your privacy above all else. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Our Privacy Core: "On-Device" Philosophy

AnyScanner is designed with a Privacy-First approach. Most of the app's core functions, including Document Scanning, OCR (Optical Character Recognition), and E-Signatures, are performed directly on your device. We do not upload your scanned documents to our servers.

2. Data Collection and Usage

To provide a seamless experience and manage your subscriptions, we process the following data:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous information about how you interact with the app (e.g., features used, screen views) to improve our service.
  • Subscription Information: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. We do not have access to your credit card details; these are handled securely by Apple.
  • Technical Data: Device model, OS version, and crash reports (via Firebase) to ensure app stability.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We partner with a limited number of trusted services to provide essential app functions:

  • Apple App Store: For payment processing and app distribution.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription validation and management.
  • Google AdMob/Unity Ads: To display ads for free users. You can manage personalized ad settings in your device’s system settings.
  • Firebase (Google): For analytics and crash reporting to improve performance.

4. Data Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
